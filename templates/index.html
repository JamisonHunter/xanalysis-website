TWITTER SENTIMENT ANALYSIS

This application performs sentiment analysis on collected Twitter data when a topic is entered to analyze.

    About This App:

  • The application gathers posts from Twitter.
  • It gathers a sample of 10 popular posts.
  • It then attributes a positive, neutral, or negative sentiment to each post.
  • These are then displayed as a graph along with a word cloud of commonly used words within the entire sample.
